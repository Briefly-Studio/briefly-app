# Briefly v1.5 — UX & Logic Improvements (Planned / In Progress)

## Purpose
Refine the v1 experience without changing architecture or introducing cloud/AI.

## Scope
- No backend
- No authentication
- No AI
- Local-only persistence

## Proposed Improvements
(derived from discussion PDFs)

### UX Improvements
- Disable quiz until deck has ≥2 cards
- Lock answers after selection
- Highlight correct/incorrect answers
- Improve empty states

### Logic Improvements
- Shuffle quiz answers per question
- Prevent duplicate distractors
- Normalize card ordering
- Safer refresh behavior after edits

### Quality of Life
- Haptic feedback on correct/incorrect
- Better microcopy
- Loading indicators

## Out of Scope
- AI generation
- Cloud sync
- Accounts
